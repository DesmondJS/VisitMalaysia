Sunway Lagoon

Sunway Lagoon is a famous multi-award-winning theme park located in Sunway City, within the district of Petaling Jaya, Selangor. Opened in 1992, it spans across an area of 88 acres and boasts over 90 attractions that spread across six adventure zones – Water Park, Amusement Park, Wildlife Park, Extreme Park, Scream Park and the Nickelodeon Lost Lagoon.
